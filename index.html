Frequently Asked Questions

Q: The app does not count my pushups automatically.

A: This is by design. The app requires you to manually enter your rep count via Digital Crown in test/AMRAP sets, or simply complete the displayed reps and tap in regular sets.

Q: My calories are showing as 0 in the Health app.

A: Make sure you have granted all HealthKit permissions. Go to iPhone → Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Pushup Guide → Enable all categories.

Q: How do I reset my level?

A: On the Home screen, long press to see the Reset option. This will reset your level and allow you to retake the Initial Test.

Q: The workouts are too hard. What can I do?

A: Reset your level (see above), then retake the Initial Test with a number slightly below your actual max reps. This sets a lower level, making the workouts more manageable. The app will automatically raise your level as you get stronger through your weekly AMRAP performance.

Q: The rest timer is not counting down accurately.

A: Go to Apple Watch → Settings → Display & Brightness → Always On → Show Apps → Turn on Pushup Guide. This keeps the screen visible and the rest timer counting accurately.
